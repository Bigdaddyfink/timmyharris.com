About Timmy Harris

My name is Timmy Harris. I am an illustrator from South Carolina. I've been doing illustration for about 30 years. I have a Bachelor of Fine Arts from Savannah College of Art and Design in illustration. I'm also a video editor, motion graphics artist, and an animator. I'm a bit of a nerd, a gamer, a connoisseur, a collector, and a filmmaker.

I have a very unique style. Which I've honed over the past 30 years. It's all about the details and the line work and it can be a bit time-consuming but I do love it. It's inspired by a lot of different things. 60s Psychedelic Poster Art, gig posters. ED "Big Daddy" Roth. Robert Williams, Art Nouveau, Art Deco. Impressionism. Pop art. Hot Rods and Beautiful Women.

I've done some work for some big clients here and there:

  • High Times Magazine
  • Greg Allman
  • Sub Cool Seeds

I've also done posters for small local bands.

Always looking to do more.
