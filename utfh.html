Urb Thrasher Flower Hour

Client Urb Thrasher Flower Hour Podcast
Screen Printed by Adam Hand & Handmade Apparel

Animation

Animation created for Urb Thrasher Flower Hour pod/video cast on the old Norml Show Live Network. The Network changed names a few times before ending its run. But Urb still does his podcast. When we designed the shirt originally we had the rocker playing a Fender Stratocaster as you can see in the animation. But Urb requested we change it to a Flying V. The animation was produced before the t-shirt.
