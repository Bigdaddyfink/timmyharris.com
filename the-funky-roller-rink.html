Big Daddy Fink's Funky Roller Rink

Seasons 01 – 03

Episodes

Examples of my video podcast from the old Norml Show Live Network / National Cannabis Radio / Russ Belville Show. It was mostly just me playing awesome music from the 60s, 70s, and 80s. Doing my best radio voice and having a dance party. It was pre recorded and I would make all the graphics that would play on screen. Hour long show played late night on Thursdays. Eventually I did them LIVE for a while before it ended.
